Our Process

Installation.

We don't just fit kitchens; we manage transformations. For homeowners who value certainty as much as aesthetics.

The "White Glove" Standard.

A high-end kitchen is a symphony of trades. Plumbers, electricians, tilers, and joiners. Without a conductor, it is chaos.

Our service is Total Project Management. We do not hand you a schedule and wish you luck. We assume full custody of the space, coordinating every trade, every delivery, and every millimetre.

We compete on craftsmanship, not speed. We measure success by the silence of the drawers and the seamlessness of the scribe.

The Timeline

From Concept to Commission.

We value your privacy and your time. This is exactly how the disruption is managed.

01

Consultation & Feasibility

We listen before we lift a tool. We assess the structural integrity, the light, and the logistics of the install.

02

Strip-Out & Preparation

Floor protection. Dust control barriers. Respect for the property is paramount during the demolition phase.

03

Installation & First Fix

The core technical work. Cabinetry levelling, stone masonry, and complex electrical integration.

04

Calibration & Details

Snagging. Appliance calibration. The final polish. Ensuring every drawer closes with a whisper.

05

Handover & Care

A complete demonstration of your appliances and advice on how to let your materials season gracefully.
