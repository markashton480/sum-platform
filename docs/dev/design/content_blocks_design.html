Case Study

The Kensington
Retrofit.

Integrating renewable technologies into heritage properties requires a delicate balance. The challenge is not merely technical, but aesthetic: how do we modernize the energy infrastructure of a Victorian terrace without disrupting its architectural soul?

The Technical Challenge

The client approached us with a specific set of constraints. The property falls within the Kensington Conservation Area, meaning any street-facing alterations were strictly prohibited.

We had to ensure complete invisibility from the street level while maximizing the southern exposure.
